TON Timeline

Late 2018

The Durov brothers, founders
of Telegram Messenger, release
the first version Telegram Open
Networck whitepaper.

2020

Telegram was forced to cease active
development of TON. A vibrant open-
source community continues
to maintain the network and build new
TON functionality, bringing Telegram s
design to fruition.

2022

TON becomes The Open Network.
TON mainnet is fully live operations,
hosting ultra-cheap fast,
and ecologically friendly transactions.
